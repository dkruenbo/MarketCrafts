[B]MarketCrafts[/B] turns custom chat channels into a server-wide crafting service board — no guild, no Discord, no add-on website required. Open the Market window, find a crafter, and whisper them in one click. Buyers can also post "Want to Buy" requests so crafters know what's in demand.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[B]What it does[/B]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Crafters opt in to advertise up to 5 recipes they are available to make — with optional notes and live cooldown timers. Buyers can browse listings in real time, search by item name, profession, or seller, open a whisper with one click, and post their own WTB requests on the Request Board.

Everything happens invisibly over a hidden custom channel ([B]MCMarket[/B]). No chat spam — the [MCR] protocol messages are silently filtered from all chat windows.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[B]Key Features[/B]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[LIST]
[*][B]Browse panel[/B] — searchable, sortable table of crafters online right now. Filter by item name, profession, or seller name. Profession filter chips let you narrow results to a single profession with one click. One-click Whisper button on every row.
[*][B]Request Board[/B] — buyers can post up to 3 "Want to Buy" requests so crafters know what's in demand. Requests expire after 30 minutes and are broadcast to all addon users.
[*][B]My Listings[/B] — advertise up to 5 recipes. Open your Spellbook → Profession window, then click [B]Add from Profession[/B] to pick directly from your recipe list. Includes a live search box for characters with hundreds of recipes. Add an optional [B]crafter note[/B] to each listing (e.g. "free if you bring mats").
[*][B]Cooldown display[/B] — if a listed recipe has a crafting cooldown (e.g. Mooncloth, Transmutes), the remaining time is broadcast and shown in the Browse panel so buyers know when you'll be ready.
[*][B]Favourite sellers[/B] — click the star icon next to any crafter to mark them as a favourite. Favourites are highlighted in the Browse panel.
[*][B]Cross-alt sync[/B] — type [B]/mc importalt[/B] on each character to save that alt's listings to your account. All alts' recipes appear under "Alt Listings" on every character's My Listings tab.
[*][B]Customisable whisper[/B] — set your own whisper template with [B]/mc template[/B]. Use {seller} and {item} placeholders to personalise the message.
[*][B]Item tooltips[/B] — hover over any listing to see the full in-game item tooltip. Shift-click to link the item into chat.
[*][B]Freshness indicators[/B] — listing rows are colour-coded green / yellow / red based on how recently the crafter was seen, so you can tell at a glance who is actively online.
[*][B]Fully opt-in[/B] — nothing is broadcast until you explicitly run [B]/mc optin[/B]. [B]/mc optout[/B] stops all broadcasting instantly.
[*][B]Zero chat spam[/B] — all protocol traffic is intercepted and hidden before it reaches any chat frame.
[*][B]Anti-abuse[/B] — rate limiting (10 messages/min per sender), max 5 listings per player, 30-minute TTL with automatic expiry.
[*][B]Blocklist[/B] — [B]/mc ignore PlayerName[/B] or right-click a listing in the Browse panel to permanently hide a player.
[*][B]Minimap button[/B] — draggable button with a tooltip showing how many crafters are currently online. Click to open the Market window.
[*][B]Automatic channel management[/B] — joins the lowest available MCMarket channel on login. Recovers gracefully if kicked. No configuration needed.
[*][B]No external dependencies[/B] — all Ace3 libraries are bundled. Drop in and go.
[*][B]Taint-safe[/B] — zero use of protected API calls; safe to use in combat.
[/LIST]

[*]Download the latest release zip.
[*]Extract the [B]MarketCrafts[/B] folder into:[LIST][*][B]World of Warcraft\_classic_tbc_\Interface\AddOns\[/B][/LIST]

[B]Quick Start[/B]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[B]As a crafter:[/B]
[LIST=1]
[*]Open your Spellbook and click a Profession to open the craft window.
[*]Type [B]/mc[/B] to open the Market window and go to the [B]My Listings[/B] tab.
[*]Click [B]Add from Profession[/B] and pick the recipes you want to advertise. Optionally add a note to each listing.
[*]Type [B]/mc optin[/B] to start broadcasting your listings to other players.
[/LIST]

[B]As a buyer:[/B]
[LIST=1]
[*]Type [B]/mc[/B] or click the minimap button to open the Market window.
[*]Use the [B]Browse[/B] tab to search for items. Filter by profession, star your favourite crafters, and hover items for tooltips.
[*]Click [B]Whisper[/B] to contact the crafter directly.
[*]Switch to the [B]Requests[/B] tab to post a "Want to Buy" request so crafters know what you need.
[/LIST]
